COMPLIANCE AND LEGAL CONTEXT

Dutch Staffing Requirements:
- WAADI registration: Maintained, displayed on website and invoices
- SNA certification: Maintained with annual audits
- CAO compliance: Wages, hours, holiday pay per collective agreement
- Reserves: Maintained for claims

GDPR:
- Controller for staff and client data
- Processor agreements with all vendors
- ROPA: Maintained, updated quarterly
- Retention periods: Defined and enforced
- Consent capture: Email, WhatsApp, SMS, cookies with timestamp
- Withdrawal: Honored immediately and synced
- Rights workflows: Access, deletion, portability, correction
- DPIA: Required before new data flows

NIS2:
- Scope assessment: Completed
- Security governance: Assigned
- Incident reporting: Within 24 hours
- Supplier management: Security requirements

Contracts:
- Staff contracts: Templated by role, auto-generated
- Client MSA + SOW: Version controlled
- Subcontractor agreements: Include WAADI, SNA, data terms

Key compliance checkpoints:
1. Verify WAADI/SNA before any staffing activity
2. Confirm consent before any outbound communication
3. Apply correct CAO rates for all roles
4. Document all data processing activities
5. Report security incidents within 24 hours
