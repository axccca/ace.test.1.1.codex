OPERATIONS AND DISPATCH CONTEXT

Shift Fill Engine:
- Tier 1: Automated matching (reliability × skills × location × cost) - Immediate
- Tier 2: WhatsApp broadcast to qualified pool - No match in 15 min
- Tier 3: Partner network activation - Internal exhausted
- Tier 4: Human dispatcher - Complex or VIP

Communication Cadence:
- Shift offered: Instant push + WhatsApp
- Confirmed: Instant email + push
- 24h before: WhatsApp reminder
- 2h before: Push reminder
- Post-shift: WhatsApp feedback request within 1 hour

No-Show Risk Model:
Inputs: Past attendance, distance, weather, day of week, role type, time since last shift
Output: Risk score 0-100
Actions: >70 = confirmation request + standby, >85 = human review + replacement
Retrain monthly on outcomes

Fill Rate Optimization:
- Overbooking 110-120% for high-risk shifts
- Standby pools pre-confirmed for peaks
- Surge incentives auto-applied for hard fills
- Geographic clustering reduces travel

Staff Performance Score:
- Attendance 40% + Punctuality 30% + Cancellation rate 20% + Feedback 10%
- Top 20%: Priority shift access
- Below threshold: Coaching → Probation
- 3+ no-shows or serious incidents: Offboarding

Cancellation Policy: Free >4h, Soft fee 4-2h, Hard fee <2h

SLA: Response within 4 hours, availability probability by city/role, credit for breaches
