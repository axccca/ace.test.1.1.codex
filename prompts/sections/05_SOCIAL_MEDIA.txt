SOCIAL MEDIA AND DISTRIBUTION CONTEXT

Platform Strategy:
- LinkedIn: B2B client acquisition, thought leadership (2-3x/week)
- Instagram: Visual employer branding, staff spotlights (3-5x/week)
- TikTok: Short recruitment content, day-in-life (3-5x/week)
- Facebook: Community, local groups (2-3x/week)
- WhatsApp Channels: Staff broadcasts (as needed)

Content Calendar (Google Sheets):
Fields: post date, platform, content type, copy, media, status
Automation: n8n trigger on scheduled date → format for platform → post via Buffer/native API → log and track

Social Listening:
- Monitor: brand mentions, competitor mentions, industry keywords
- Aggregate sentiment scores
- Alert on spikes (positive or negative)
- Route engagement opportunities within 30 minutes
- Track response times

UGC Collection:
- Post-shift WhatsApp: Request photo/video testimonial
- Route to review queue
- Approved content: schedule with credits and consent logged
- Maintain UGC library by type, city, role

Job Posting Syndication:
- New role in CRM → formatted social posts
- Add city targeting and role hashtags
- Publish with UTM tracking
- Track applications by source

Content Themes:
- Behind-the-scenes at venues
- Staff success stories
- Day-in-the-life content
- Tips for horeca workers
- City-specific content
- Compliance/professionalism highlights
