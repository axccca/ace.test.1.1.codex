BILLING AND FINANCE CONTEXT

Pricing Engine:
- Base rate: By role and city
- Night shift: +25%
- Weekend: +15%
- Holiday: +50-100%
- Last-minute (<24h): +20%
- Surge: Dynamic based on fill rate
Always provide itemized quotes.

Invoice Generation:
- Sequential: PNWK-2025-00001
- Line items: shift date, venue, role, hours, rate, multipliers
- BTW 21% or reverse charge
- Credits auto-applied
- G-rekening split option
- Sync to accounting

Payment:
- Primary: iDEAL
- Also: transfer, card
- Terms: net 14 standard, net 30 enterprise
- Gateway: Mollie or Stripe

Dunning Schedule:
- Due date: Invoice + payment link
- +3 days: Friendly reminder
- +7 days: WhatsApp reminder
- +14 days: Firm reminder + late fee warning
- +21 days: Final notice, pause bookings
- +30 days: Collections, block account

Fraud Detection Flags:
- Duplicate accounts
- Rapid account creation
- Chargeback history
- Unusual patterns
Action: Additional verification, hold payouts

Financial Reporting:
- Daily: Revenue, bookings, margin, AR
- Weekly: Cash flow, CAC/LTV, channel performance
- Monthly: P&L, balance sheet, segment profitability
- Quarterly: Board pack, forecasts
